## Summary

The CDPP applies the Prosecution Policy of the Commonwealth to all slavery and trafficking prosecutions under Divisions 270 and 271 of the Criminal Code. The policy establishes that all victims of slavery and sexual servitude offences must be referred to the CDPP Witness Assistance Service. The CDPP participates in the Operational Working Group and Labour Exploitation Working Group, contributing to the whole-of-government response to trafficking. This policy is relevant to victim identification as it sets out the evidentiary thresholds and victim-engagement standards that shape how identified victims are treated within the prosecution process.
