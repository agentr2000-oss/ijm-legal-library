  CPS practice guidance implementing the non-punishment principle for trafficking victims.
  Directs prosecutors to identify potential trafficking victims before making prosecution
  decisions, applying public interest considerations and the s. 45 defence. Requires that
  where a credible defence of trafficking-compelled offending exists, the case should not
  be charged or should be discontinued.
