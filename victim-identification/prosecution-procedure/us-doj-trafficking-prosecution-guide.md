## Summary

DOJ prosecution guidance for federal human trafficking cases, issued by the Human Trafficking Prosecution Unit (HTPU) within the Civil Rights Division. Includes victim identification procedures to be followed during investigation and prosecution, guidance on applying force/fraud/coercion elements, and coordination protocols with victim service providers.

## Key Provisions
