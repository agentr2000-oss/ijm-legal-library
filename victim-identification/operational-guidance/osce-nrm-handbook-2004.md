## Summary

Foundational handbook on designing and implementing National Referral Mechanisms, defining the concept, structures, and roles of governmental and civil-society actors in a multi-agency identification and referral framework. Establishes the NRM model now adopted across the OSCE region and beyond, covering identification procedures, referral pathways, and minimum standards for victim assistance.
