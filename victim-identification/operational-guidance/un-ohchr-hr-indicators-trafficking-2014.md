## Summary

Framework of structural, process, and outcome indicators for assessing the quality and human-rights compliance of national anti-trafficking responses. Includes specific indicators for measuring whether victim identification systems are accessible, non-discriminatory, and effective, enabling States to benchmark and improve their identification mechanisms.

## Key Provisions

Indicator framework: structural, process, and outcome indicators applied to trafficking prevention, protection, and prosecution
