## Summary

Practical identification guide for front-line police officers, enhancing skills to recognize victims of trafficking beyond sexual exploitation to include domestic servitude, labour exploitation in construction, agriculture, and garment industries. Integrates community policing approaches with anti-trafficking expertise to support proactive identification and appropriate initial response to potential trafficking victims encountered during routine policing.

## Key Provisions

Identification indicators by exploitation type; Initial response procedures; Community policing integration
