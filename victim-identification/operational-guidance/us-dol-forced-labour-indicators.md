## Summary

Guidance from the DOL Wage and Hour Division on identifying human trafficking during routine workplace investigations. WHD investigators are often the first federal agency to contact vulnerable workers; the guidance addresses labour trafficking indicators in high-risk industries, protocols for referring suspected trafficking victims, and the division's role in certifying U visa requests for qualifying criminal activities including forced labour and fraud in foreign labour contracting.

## Key Provisions

Labour trafficking indicators for workplace investigators; U visa certification process
