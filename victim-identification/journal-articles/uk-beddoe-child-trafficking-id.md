## Summary

Foundational research on identifying child trafficking victims in three English regions, documenting 80 known or suspected child trafficking cases. Found that 60% of identified child victims went missing from care and were never recovered, highlighting critical failures in the child protection system's ability to identify and safeguard trafficked children.

## Key Provisions

Findings on child trafficking indicators, 60% missing-from-care rate, and recommendations for child protection agencies
