## Summary

Independent review commissioned by the Home Office examining the effectiveness of the Modern Slavery Act 2015 and the National Referral Mechanism in identifying victims. Assesses inconsistencies in NRM decision-making across regions, first responder awareness gaps, and makes recommendations for improving the speed and quality of victim identification.
