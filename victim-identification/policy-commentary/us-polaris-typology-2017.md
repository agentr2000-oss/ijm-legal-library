## Summary

Analysis of over 32,000 cases documented through the National Human Trafficking Hotline (2007–2016), defining 25 distinct types of human trafficking in the United States. Each type includes specific trafficker profiles, recruitment tactics, victim profiles, and methods of control, providing a granular identification framework that moves beyond broad sex/labour trafficking categories.
