## Summary

Survey-based assessment of the status and effectiveness of National Referral Mechanisms across OSCE participating States, documenting variations in NRM design, competent-authority structures, and identification procedures. Provides a comparative overview essential for understanding regional best practices and gaps in multi-agency victim identification systems.

## Key Provisions
