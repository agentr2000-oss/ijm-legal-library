## Summary

Labour trafficking case in which the Ninth Circuit examined the coercion analysis under 18 USC § 1589 (forced labour). The court addressed what constitutes a "scheme, plan, or pattern" intended to cause belief of serious harm, providing guidance on identifying victims of labour trafficking through coercive control indicators.
