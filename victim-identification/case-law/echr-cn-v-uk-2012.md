summary: >
  The Court held that the United Kingdom violated Article 4 by lacking a specific
  legislative provision criminalising domestic servitude at the relevant time. The
  judgment underscored the procedural limb of Art. 4, requiring states to maintain
  an adequate legal framework enabling effective investigation of credible claims of
  servitude and to identify potential victims through that process.
