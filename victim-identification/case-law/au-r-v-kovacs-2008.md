summary: >
  Queensland Court of Appeal decision involving the first successful trafficking prosecution outside the sex industry in Australia. The case concerned a Filipino woman brought to Australia through a sham marriage who was subjected to domestic servitude and repeated sexual assault. The decision expanded the application of slavery and trafficking offences to forms of exploitation beyond the commercial sex industry, establishing important precedent for identifying victims of domestic servitude and forced labour facilitated through immigration fraud.
