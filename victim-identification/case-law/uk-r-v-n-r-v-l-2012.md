## Summary

Foundational Court of Appeal guidance on the treatment of potential trafficking victims in the criminal justice system. Held that courts must investigate trafficking indicators before convicting potential victims, and that the CPS should apply the Palermo Protocol and ECAT obligations when deciding whether to prosecute. Precursor to the s. 45 statutory defence introduced by the Modern Slavery Act 2015.

## Key Provisions

Paras. 7–13 (duty to investigate trafficking indicators), paras. 20–24 (CPS obligations and Article 26 ECAT)
