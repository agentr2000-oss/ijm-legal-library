## Summary

First Article 4 case in which the ECtHR found a violation for domestic servitude, distinguishing servitude from slavery and forced labour. The Court held that France failed to afford the applicant — a Togolese minor compelled to work as an unpaid domestic servant — practical and effective protection under its criminal law framework, establishing that states must criminalise and effectively punish conduct contrary to Art. 4.

## Key Provisions

Paras. 112–149 (definition of servitude; positive obligation to penalise and prosecute)
