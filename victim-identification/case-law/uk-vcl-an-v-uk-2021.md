summary: >
  The ECtHR held that the UK violated Article 4 ECHR by failing to identify two Vietnamese
  children as victims of trafficking before prosecuting them for cannabis cultivation offences.
  Establishes that once authorities are aware of a credible suspicion of trafficking, the
  individual must be assessed by a qualified person before any prosecution decision is taken.
