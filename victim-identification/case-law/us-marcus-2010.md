## Summary

Sex trafficking conviction involving online exploitation. The Second Circuit analysed the force, fraud, and coercion indicators required under 18 USC § 1591 and addressed evidentiary sufficiency for victim identification in sex trafficking prosecutions, including the role of psychological coercion.
