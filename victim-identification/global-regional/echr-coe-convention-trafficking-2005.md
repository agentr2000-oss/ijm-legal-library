## Summary

The foundational Council of Europe treaty on combating human trafficking, establishing the framework for victim identification (Art. 10), the National Referral Mechanism model (Art. 10), a minimum 30-day recovery and reflection period (Art. 13), residence permits for victims (Art. 14), and the non-punishment principle (Art. 26). Articles 10-16 set out detailed obligations on identification, assistance, and protection of trafficking victims.

## Key Provisions

Arts. 10–16 (identification, NRM, reflection period, assistance); Art. 26 (non-punishment)
