## Summary

Updates Convention No. 29 with modern obligations for prevention, protection, and remedies. Requires States to take measures for the identification, release, protection, and recovery of victims of forced labour, including trafficking victims, and mandates access to appropriate and effective remedies.

## Key Provisions

Arts. 1–4 (prevention, protection, victim identification and access to remedies)
